Import and Distribution of Automotive Lubricants

We import and distribute top-tier automotive lubricants under our Stay Power brand as well as Aisin and Oscar, ensuring reliable and efficient performance for all types of vehicles.

Exclusive Supply of Aisin Lubricants

As the sole distributor of Aisin lubricants in Africa, we provide high-quality engine oil, gear oil, and automatic transmission fluid to keep vehicles running smoothly and efficiently.

Wide Range of Automotive Products

In addition to lubricants, our Stay Power brand includes a variety of automotive essentials such as Bostic car batteries, sealants, gasket makers, AB 80, carburetor cleaners, and epoxy.

Industrial-Grade Solutions

We offer products like Oscar oils in large 208L drums, catering to industrial clients and providing bulk solutions for large-scale automotive operations.

Superior Customer Service

Our team is dedicated to providing top-notch customer service, ensuring all clients receive expert advice and support in selecting the best products for their needs.

Efficient Logistics and Inventory Management

We maintain a robust supply chain and inventory management system to guarantee timely delivery and availability of products across the continent.
