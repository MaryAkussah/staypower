ABOUT US

We offer a diverse range of
high-quality products across three leading brands:

1. Stay Power: Our in-house brand features an extensive line of products, including Bostic car batteries, sealants, gasket makers, AB 80, carburetor cleaners, and epoxy. Stay Power is synonymous with reliability and high performance.
2. Aisin: As the sole supplier of Aisin lubricants in Africa, we provide top-tier car lubricants, including engine oil, gear oil, and automatic transmission fluid, ensuring superior engine performance and longevity.
3. Oscar: We also carry Oscar lubricants, offering engine oil, gear oil, and automatic transmission fluid for all car types. Oscar oils are available in various sizes, including large 208L drums, catering to both individual and industrial clients.
